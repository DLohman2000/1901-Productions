Welcome to 1901 Productions

We are a creative and innovative Student-Run Venture film production company dedicated to producing quality low-budget films that entertain and inspire. From pre-production to post-production, we handle every aspect of the filmmaking process with professionalism and passion.

Featured Films

SONDER is an anthology of four short films; “Loss of Self”, “Sword and the Stone”, “Dissociate”, and “Darkroom”. The anthology follows four strangers and the complexities of their life and what they each lost. 

Unlived and Making the Dead

Unlived and Making the Dead are our next two installments of our next Anthology revolving around horror. Unlived tells a story of Moss who wakes up one day and his friends don't notice him. We follow him on his journey to figure out why and what happened. Making the Dead is a comedy horror following around a film crew when something unexpected happens on set.
