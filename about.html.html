About Us

 Over the past few years 1901 has worked on developing short films, averaging 30 minutes, and contract work, working with professional standard equipment! 1901 Productions has allowed the students from Arts Administration and Arts Technology and the School of Theatre and Dance to go through the pre-production, production and post-production phases of a film allowing them to learn, grow, and get better each time.
